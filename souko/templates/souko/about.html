About Me

Jacob Cuke

Hello! My name is Jacob Cuke and I am a graduate of Computer Science from the University of Calgary. I currently work as a full stack web developer, and hope to one day live and work in Japan.

MySouko

Over the past 5 years I have taught myself Japanese to fluency, and have also studied both Computer Science and Japanese culture entirely in Japanese at Senshu University in Tokyo. I was in the middle of an exchange program in Japan when the COVID-19 pandemic first hit in March 2020, forcing me to come back to Canada earlier than expected.

In order to maintain and further improve my Japanese in the midst of the lockdown, I began consuming Japanese books, TV shows and games in my spare time. In addition to being a hobby that I enjoy, this practice has allowed me to gain a deep understanding of the Japanese language, to the point where reading Japanese has become almost as effortless as reading English.

MySouko, comes from the Japanese word 倉庫(souko) meaning storehouse or warehouse. This site was an attempt to combine my love of Japanese and web development, to create a platform where I could keep a record of all the Japanese media I have consumed since beginning this endeavour in March 2020.
