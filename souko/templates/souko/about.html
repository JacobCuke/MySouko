About Me

Jacob Cuke

Hello! My name is Jacob Cuke and I am an undergraduate in Computer Science at the University of Calgary, graduating in May of 2021. I focus mainly on web application development and database management.

MySouko

Over the past 4 years I have taught myself Japanese to fluency, and have studied both Computer Science and Japanese culture at Senshu University in Tokyo. I was in the middle of an exchange program in Japan when the COVID-19 pandemic first hit in March 2020, forcing me to come back to Canada earlier than expected.

In order to maintain and further improve my Japanese in the midst of the lockdown, I began consuming Japanese books and TV shows on a daily basis. In addition to being a hobby that I enjoy, it allowed me to reach a point where reading Japanese has become almost as effortless as reading English.

MySouko, comes from the Japanese word 倉庫(souko) meaning storehouse or warehouse. This site was an attempt to combine my love of Japanese and web development, to create a platform where I could keep a record of all the Japanese media I have consumed since beginning this endeavour in March 2020.
